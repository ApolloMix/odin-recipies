Chicken Parmesan

Description

This one is a bit more complicated if you don't have cooking experience. Unlike spaghetti which is simple, you're going to have to do a little bit of wisking and mixing ingredients. Regardless, making this dish is surely worth it.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Eggs
  • Panko crumbs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Prep raw chicken
  2. Add panko crumps to wisking bowl
  3. Add eggs to panko crumbs and wisk thoroughly
  4. Apply mixture to chicken by rolling raw chicken around in the bowl
  5. Cook chicken
  6. Cook tomato sauce in pan
  7. When chicken is done, add tomato sauce to chicken
  8. Add parmesean cheese to the top of the chicken
  9. Enjoy!
