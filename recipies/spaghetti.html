Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is one of the simplest dishes a person can make. Everyone I know who doesn't know how to cook can make spaghetti. Follow these simple steps to create a great bowl/plate of some spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • One stalk of celery, chopped
  • One diced tomato
  • One can of tomato paste
  • One can of mushrooms/li>
  • Five cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 Cup of parsley
  • Oregano
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Put noodles in boiling water
  3. Heat pan and add beef to pan
  4. In separate pan, add mushrooms, garlic, and tomato sauce
  5. Chop garlic and add it to sauce after letting it simmer
  6. Add meat to sauce and stir
  7. Once noodles are finished, add meat and sauce to pot and stir
  8. Serve on a plate or bowl, and enjoy!
