Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken alfedo is one of the greatest dishes that man has ever produced. With this quick recipe of how to cook chicken alfredo, you will be treating yourself and yourself and your family to a true declicacy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Noodles
  • Green onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps
  1. Wash your hands!
  2. Prep chicken
  3. WASH YOUR HANDS!!
  4. Boil water in pot
  5. Add noodles to boiling water
  6. Start cooking chicken
  7. Start cooking onions, this will be quick so put them off to the side after they are done
  8. When noodles are done, drain water
  9. Add oninons to pot
  10. When chicken is done, add chicken to pot
  11. Add sauce and stir
  12. Season with salt/pepper as needed
  13. Enjoy!
